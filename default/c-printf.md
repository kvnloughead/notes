## Formatting

`%d` - integer
`%6d` - integer, takes up at least 6 spaces

`%f` - float
`%6f` - float, takes up at least 6 spaces
`%6.1f` - float, 6 spaces, 1 significant digit

`%o` - octal
`%x` - hexadecimal
`%c` - character
`%s` - string
`%%` - `%`

- width and precision can both be omitted
